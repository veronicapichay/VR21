The inspiration behind this piece was Fall foliage. Deep red being one of my favorite colors for the fall, I decided to use this as the background. Being that it's a dark color, it's easy to layer on different colors that will coordinate well, while adding a pop to it.

Around this time I had been making a few more "simpler" and "cute" pieces, so I wanted to being myself back to making something a little bit more abstract. Although semi simple in design, from afar, the origami pieces appear a bit obscure, almost reminiscent of a pile of leaves. Looking closely, we can see that the origami is in fact fish swimming in all different directions.
